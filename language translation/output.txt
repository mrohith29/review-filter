In Hyderabad, a city in Bharat, how are you people speak both Hindi and Telugu. How are you? They say hello for hello and Thank you for thank you in Hindi. In Telugu, they say Namaskaramu and thank you for the same. They love to eat Biryani, a popular dish in both cultures. The city is known for its Charminar, a historical monument. People there celebrate festivals like Diwali, known as Deepavali in Telugu, with great joy. They believe in Guest Devo Bhava, a Sanskrit phrase meaning 'The guest is equivalent to God'. This multicultural city is a beautiful example of unity in diversity.
